# FragmentFi: Democratizing Institutional Yield Through Tokenized Real-World Assets

## Executive Summary

For decades, access to safe, yield-generating assets such as Treasury Bills, money market funds, and institutional fixed-income products has been reserved for banks, funds, and wealthy investors.

A student with $5 cannot invest in the same products as a hedge fund. A young professional with $50 cannot access institutional-grade yields. The financial system was never designed for micro-investors.

Meanwhile, the tokenized Real World Asset (RWA) sector is experiencing explosive growth, with hundreds of millions of dollars in tokenized treasury products already live on blockchain networks.

FragmentFi changes this.

Built on Stellar, FragmentFi allows anyone to gain fractional exposure to yield-bearing assets through transparent, on-chain ownership tokens. Users can invest from as little as $1, receive tokenized ownership, track their portfolio in real time, and earn automated yield distributions.

We are building the retail gateway to the future of tokenized finance.

# The Problem

## Institutional Yield is Locked Behind Barriers

Today, institutional investors enjoy access to:

* Treasury Bills
* Money Market Funds
* Fixed Income Products
* Cash Management Funds

These assets are considered some of the safest and most reliable yield-generating instruments in the world.

Yet retail investors face major limitations:

* High minimum investments
* Geographic restrictions
* Brokerage requirements
* Complex onboarding
* Limited transparency

As a result, millions of people keep their money idle while institutional capital continues earning predictable returns.

The future of finance should not depend on account size.

# The Solution: FragmentFi

FragmentFi transforms real-world assets into fractional digital ownership tokens.

Instead of requiring $1,000 or $10,000 to participate, users can start with as little as $1.

### Example

Rahul wants exposure to treasury-backed assets.

Instead of opening multiple accounts and navigating complex financial products:

1. Rahul deposits $10
2. FragmentFi mints 10 FRAG tokens
3. Each FRAG token represents fractional ownership of the underlying asset pool
4. Yield is distributed automatically
5. Rahul watches his balance grow transparently

The process is simple, transparent, and accessible to anyone.

---

# How It Works

## Step 1: Deposit

Users connect their Stellar wallet and deposit supported assets.

Example:

$10 Deposit → 10 FRAG Tokens

---

## Step 2: Fractional Ownership

FragmentFi issues FRAG tokens representing ownership within the asset pool.

Every token is recorded on-chain and can be independently verified.

Users always know:

* What they own
* How much they own
* What backs their investment

---

## Step 3: Yield Distribution

The underlying asset pool generates yield.

FragmentFi distributes rewards proportionally to token holders on a weekly basis.

Example:

Pool Yield: $100

User Ownership: 5%

Reward Received: $5

Everything is transparent and auditable.
